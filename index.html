About Me

Hello! My name is **FoxxDev**, and I am a programmer specializing in **Roblox Studio** and **web application development**. Over the years, I’ve developed a strong passion for creating immersive games and experiences on the Roblox platform, as well as building modern web solutions.

Currently, I am working on an exciting project: a **microblogging social network**, which aims to connect people and allow them to share their ideas quickly and creatively, focusing on simplicity and interaction.

Projects

Roblox Studio: Games and Tools

In addition to social experiences, I also create games on **Roblox Studio**. My projects include open worlds, adventures, and tools to help other developers create their own Roblox experiences.
